High-Quality Screws for Reliable and Secure Fastening

At Mum-Supplies, we offer a comprehensive range of high-performance screws designed for strength, durability, and precision. Our screws provide secure fastening in various materials, including wood, metal, plastic, drywall, and masonry. Whether you need screws for construction, carpentry, manufacturing, or DIY projects, we have the right solution for every application. With advanced thread designs, corrosion-resistant finishes, and precision engineering, our screws ensure maximum holding power in all conditions.

Screws – Product Specifications

Materials:

  • ✔ Stainless Steel (304, 316) – High corrosion resistance for outdoor and marine use
  • ✔ Carbon Steel – High-strength and durable for heavy-duty applications
  • ✔ Alloy Steel – Enhanced hardness and wear resistance
  • ✔ Brass & Copper – Non-magnetic and corrosion-resistant for electrical and decorative use
  • ✔ Zinc-Plated & Galvanized Steel – Rust-resistant coatings for extended lifespan

Types of Screws:

  • ✔ Wood Screws – Designed for secure fastening in softwood, hardwood, and MDF
  • ✔ Self-Tapping Screws – Create their own threads for easy installation in metal and plastic
  • ✔ Machine Screws – Precision-threaded for metal-to-metal fastening
  • ✔ Drywall Screws – Sharp-pointed for strong grip in plasterboard and drywall applications
  • ✔ Decking Screws – Corrosion-resistant and weatherproof for outdoor decking projects
  • ✔ Masonry Screws – Designed for drilling into brick, concrete, and stone
  • ✔ Socket Screws – High-torque fasteners with hexagonal socket heads

Thread & Drive Types:

  • ✔ Coarse & Fine Threads – For different load-bearing and grip requirements
  • ✔ Self-Tapping & Self-Drilling – For fast and easy installations
  • ✔ Metric & Imperial Threads – Standardized for universal compatibility
  • ✔ Drive Types: Phillips, Slotted, Torx, Hex, and Pozidriv

Sizes & Dimensions:

  • ✔ Available in a variety of diameters, lengths, and thread configurations
  • ✔ Custom sizes available upon request

Coatings & Finishes:

  • ✔ Zinc-Plated – General-purpose corrosion resistance
  • ✔ Hot-Dip Galvanized – Heavy-duty rust protection for outdoor applications
  • ✔ Black Oxide – Provides minimal corrosion resistance with a sleek finish
  • ✔ Nickel & Chrome-Plated – Enhances aesthetics and durability
  • ✔ PTFE & Specialty Coatings – Improved lubrication and chemical resistance

Standards & Certifications:

  • ✔ ISO, DIN, ANSI, ASTM, BS & JIS Compliance
  • ✔ RoHS & REACH Compliant – Environmentally safe coatings
  • ✔ High-Tensile & Industrial-Grade Screws Available
